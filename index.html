A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Ann Arbor, MI. My journey into tech started with a degree in Communication Studies at The University of Michigan. I have always been interested in problem solving and how to communicate clearly with words and imagery.

I work on a freelance basis with a particular focus on bringing the dreams of small business owners into reality. Building user-friendly ways for my clients to communicate their message and help their customers take action makes me very happy.
